## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to the Application**
   - Open your web browser and go to `http://localhost:3000`

2. **Sign Up**
   - Click on "Sign Up" or "Register"
   - Fill in your details:
     - Full Name
     - Email Address
     - Password
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Sign In**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"
   - You'll be redirected to your home feed

### First Time Setup

After creating your account, you should:
1. Complete your profile (see [Profile Setup](#profile-setup))
2. Add profile and cover photos
3. Add your work experience and education
4. Add skills to showcase your expertise

## Account & Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your profile picture in the top navigation bar
2. Select "View Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile displays:
   - Profile and cover photos
   - Headline/title
   - Location
   - About section
   - Experience
   - Education
   - Skills
   - Accomplishments

### Profile Setup

#### Adding Basic Information

1. **Edit Profile (Basic Info)**
   - On your profile page, look for the pencil/edit icon (‚úèÔ∏è) on the top right of your profile card
   - Click the "Edit" button
   - A dialog will open where you can update:
     - **Name** - Your full name
     - **Professional Title/Headline** - Your current role or professional identity
     - **Location** - Your city and country/state
     - **About/Bio** - A summary of your professional background (supports multiple lines)
   - Click "Save" to update your profile
   - Changes appear immediately on your profile

2. **Update Profile Photo and Cover Photo**

   You can now update your photos directly from the Edit Profile dialog:

   **Steps:**
   - Click the pencil/edit icon (‚úèÔ∏è) on your profile
   - Scroll down to the "Profile Images" section
   - You'll see two fields:
     - **Profile Photo URL**: Enter a direct link to your profile picture
     - **Cover Photo URL**: Enter a direct link to your cover image
   - As you type, a preview will appear below each field
   - Click "Save" when done

   **Where to Get Image URLs:**

   **Free Placeholder Services:**
   - Profile photos: `https://i.pravatar.cc/300` (random avatar)
   - Cover photos: `https://picsum.photos/1584/396` (random banner)

   **Your Own Images:**
   1. Upload your photo to an image hosting service:
      - **Imgur** (https://imgur.com) - Free, no account needed
      - **Cloudinary** (https://cloudinary.com) - Professional hosting
      - **Google Photos** - Share and get public link
   2. Get the **direct image URL** (must end in .jpg, .png, .gif, etc.)
   3. Paste the URL into the Edit Profile dialog
   4. Preview appears automatically
   5. Click Save

   **Image Recommendations:**
   - Profile Photo: 400x400px (square format works best)
   - Cover Photo: 1584x396px (wide banner, like LinkedIn)
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
   - Use professional, high-quality images
   - File size: Keep under 2MB for fast loading

   **Quick Example:**
   - Open Edit Profile
   - In "Profile Photo URL" field, paste: `https://i.pravatar.cc/300`
   - In "Cover Photo URL" field, paste: `https://picsum.photos/1584/396`
   - See instant previews
   - Click Save

#### Adding Experience

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Scroll to the "Experience" section
3. Click the "+" (Add) button
4. Fill in the details:
   - Job Title
   - Company Name
   - Location
   - Start Date
   - End Date (or check "I currently work here")
   - Description of your role and achievements
5. Click "Save"

**Tips for Experience:**
- Use bullet points for responsibilities
- Quantify achievements when possible
- Include relevant technologies or skills used
- Keep descriptions concise but informative

#### Adding Education

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Scroll to the "Education" section
3. Click the "+" (Add) button
4. Fill in the details:
   - School/University Name
   - Degree
   - Field of Study
   - Start Year
   - End Year (or check "Currently studying")
   - Activities and societies (optional)
5. Click "Save"

#### Adding Skills

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Scroll to the "Skills" section
3. Click "Add Skill" or the "+" button
4. Type your skill name
5. Press Enter or click "Add"
6. Repeat for multiple skills

**Recommended:**
- Add at least 3-5 core skills
- Focus on professional/technical skills
- Skills help others find you in searches

#### Adding Accomplishments

Click "Add Section" and choose from:

1. **Languages**
   - Language name
   - Proficiency level (Native, Professional, Limited)

2. **Certifications**
   - Certification name
   - Issuing organization
   - Issue date
   - Expiration date (if applicable)
   - Credential URL

3. **Projects**
   - Project name
   - Description
   - Date
   - Associated URL

4. **Publications**
   - Publication title
   - Publisher
   - Publication date
   - URL

5. **Honors & Awards**
   - Award name
   - Issuer
   - Date received
   - Description

### Profile Completeness

Your profile has a **Profile Strength** widget that shows:
- Completion percentage (0-100%)
- Color-coded progress bar:
  - Red (0-49%): Needs attention
  - Yellow (50-79%): Good progress
  - Green (80-100%): Strong profile
- List of missing sections to complete

**To improve your profile strength:**
1. Check the expandable list of missing items
2. Click to expand and see what's needed
3. Add each missing section
4. Refresh to see updated completion percentage

## Creating Content

### Creating a Text Post

1. **Open Post Creator:**
   - Click "Start a post" in the create post box
   - The post creation dialog opens

2. **Write Your Post:**
   - Type your content in the text area
   - Maximum characters may apply
   - Line breaks are supported

3. **Add Emojis:**
   - Click the emoji button (üòä) in the toolbar
   - Browse or search for emojis
   - Click an emoji to insert it at cursor position
   - Emoji picker closes automatically

4. **Post:**
   - Click the "Post" button
   - Your post appears in your feed

**Tips for Great Posts:**
- Keep it concise and engaging
- Use emojis strategically
- Ask questions to encourage engagement
- Share insights or valuable information
- Proofread before posting

### Creating a Photo Post

1. Click "Photo" from the main feed
2. Write your caption/text
3. Click "Click to upload photo"
4. Select an image file from your computer
5. Preview appears - you can remove and re-upload
6. Click "Post"

**Photo Best Practices:**
- Use high-quality images
- Recommended size: 1200x627px
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG
- Keep file size reasonable for faster loading

### Creating a Video Post

1. Click "Video" from the main feed
2. Write your caption/text
3. Click "Click to upload video"
4. Select a video file from your computer
5. Preview appears
6. Click "Post"

**Video Tips:**
- Keep videos under 10 minutes for best engagement
- Add captions in your post text
- Ensure good lighting and audio quality
- Supported formats: MP4, MOV

### Creating a Poll

1. Click "Poll" from the main feed
2. Enter your poll question
3. Add poll options:
   - Minimum 2 options
   - Maximum 4 options
   - Click "+ Add option" for more
4. Click "Post"

**Poll Best Practices:**
- Ask clear, specific questions
- Keep options mutually exclusive
- Use polls to gather opinions or feedback
- Engage with voters in comments

### Writing an Article

1. Click "Write article" from the main feed
2. Enter article title (first line)
3. Write your content
4. Optional: Add a cover image
5. Click "Post"

**Article Features:**
- Longer-form content than regular posts
- First line becomes the article title
- First 200 characters used as excerpt
- Articles have their own dedicated view
- Readers can like and engage

### Attaching Documents

1. Open the post creator
2. Click the attachment icon (üìé)
3. Select a document file:
   - PDF
   - Word (.doc, .docx)
   - PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx)
   - Excel (.xls, .xlsx)
   - Text files (.txt)
4. File preview shows name and size
5. Add your post text
6. Click "Post"

**Document Sharing:**
- Great for sharing presentations, reports, or PDFs
- Recipients can view document details
- File size displayed automatically

## Stories

### Viewing Stories

1. **Stories Carousel:**
   - Located at the top of your home feed
   - Circular avatars with gradient borders indicate active stories
   - Your own story appears first with a "+" icon

2. **Watch a Story:**
   - Click on any story circle
   - Full-screen story viewer opens
   - Features:
     - Progress bars at top show story count
     - Auto-advances after 5 seconds
     - Click left to go back
     - Click right to go forward
     - Click X to close

3. **Story Navigation:**
   - Stories auto-advance within a user's stories
   - After finishing one user's stories, moves to next user
   - Tap/click left/right sides to manually navigate

### Creating a Story

1. **Open Story Creator:**
   - Click "Create story" (your avatar with + icon)
   - Story creation dialog opens

2. **Choose Story Type:**

   **Image Story:**
   - Click "Image" tab
   - Enter image URL
   - Preview appears below
   - Click "Share Story"

   **Video Story:**
   - Click "Video" tab
   - Enter video URL
   - Click "Share Story"

   **Text Story:**
   - Click "Text" tab
   - Type your message (max 300 characters)
   - Character count shown
   - Appears with gradient background
   - Click "Share Story"

3. **Story Expiration:**
   - All stories expire after 24 hours
   - No notification when expired
   - Stories automatically removed from feed

**Story Best Practices:**
- Keep text stories short and impactful
- Use high-quality images/videos
- Share timely, relevant content
- Professional content only
- Stories show to all your connections

## Networking

### Managing Your Network

Navigate to "My Network" in the top navigation.

**Network Sections:**
- **Invitations:** Pending connection requests
- **Connections:** Your accepted connections
- **Suggestions:** People you may know
- **Following:** Companies and influencers you follow
- **Groups:** Professional groups you've joined
- **Events:** Upcoming events
- **Pages:** Company pages you follow

### Sending Connection Requests

1. **From People You May Know:**
   - Navigate to "My Network"
   - Browse suggested connections
   - Each card shows:
     - Name and profile photo
     - Current job title
     - Mutual connections count
   - Click "Connect" button
   - Request sent immediately

2. **From Search:**
   - Use search bar to find people
   - Click on their profile
   - Click "Connect" button

3. **Connection Best Practices:**
   - Only connect with people you know or have interacted with
   - Personalize connection requests when possible
   - Review mutual connections before connecting

### Managing Connection Requests

1. **View Invitations:**
   - Go to "My Network"
   - "Invitations" section shows pending requests
   - Each shows sender's name, title, and photo

2. **Accept Request:**
   - Click "Accept" button
   - User immediately added to connections
   - Both users notified

3. **Ignore Request:**
   - Click "Ignore" button
   - Request removed from list
   - Sender not notified

### Viewing Your Connections

1. Navigate to "My Network"
2. Click "Connections" in the left sidebar
3. View all accepted connections
4. Each connection shows:
   - Name and photo
   - Current title and company
   - Location
   - Connection date
5. Click "Message" to start a conversation

### People You May Know

The system suggests connections based on:
- Shared connections
- Similar industries or roles
- Common interests
- Location proximity
- Recent profile views

**To see suggestions:**
1. Go to "My Network"
2. Default view shows "People you may know"
3. Updated regularly with new suggestions

### Following Companies and Influencers

1. Navigate to "Following & followers" section
2. View companies and influencers you follow
3. See follower counts
4. Click "Following" to manage or unfollow

## Messaging

### Accessing Messages

1. Click the messaging icon in the top navigation
2. Or navigate to "/messaging" in the URL
3. Messages page displays:
   - Left: Conversation list
   - Right: Active conversation

### Starting a New Conversation

1. **From Connections:**
   - Go to "My Network" > "Connections"
   - Find the person you want to message
   - Click "Message" button
   - Message window opens

2. **From Profile:**
   - Visit a connection's profile
   - Click "Message" button
   - Redirected to messaging page

### Sending Messages

1. **Select Conversation:**
   - Click on a conversation from the list
   - Or start a new conversation

2. **Type Your Message:**
   - Click the message input field at bottom
   - Type your message
   - Press Enter or click Send

3. **Message Features:**
   - Real-time message delivery
   - Read receipts (shows if message is read)
   - Timestamp on each message
   - Message history preserved

### Managing Conversations

- **Conversation List:**
  - Shows most recent conversations first
  - Unread messages highlighted
  - Search conversations using search bar
  - Avatar and name shown for each contact

- **Active Conversation:**
  - Scrollable message history
  - Most recent at bottom
  - Your messages aligned right
  - Their messages aligned left

1. **Open Job Details:**

1. **Navigate to Jobs Page:**
   - Click "Jobs" in navigation

2. **Fill Job Details:**
   - **Basic Information:**
     - Job Title
     - Company Name
     - Location

     - Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship, etc.)

     - Requirements (skills, education, experience)

3. **Post the Job:**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Sign In:**
- Verify email and password
- Check caps lock
- Try password reset

**Profile Won't Update:**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page and try again

**Posts Not Appearing:**
- Check if post was successfully created
- Refresh your feed
- Verify you're logged in

**Messages Not Sending:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify recipient is a connection
- Refresh messaging page

**Job Application Failed:**
- Ensure all required fields completed
- Check file upload size limits
- Verify job is still open

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check console for errors
5. Contact system administrator

## Glossary

**Connection:** A professional relationship between two users who have accepted each other's connection requests.

**Feed:** The main timeline showing posts from your connections and suggested content.

**Story:** Temporary content that disappears after 24 hours, similar to Instagram Stories.

**Reaction:** An emoji-based response to a post (Like, Celebrate, Support, Love, Insightful, Curious).

**Profile Strength:** A percentage indicating how complete your profile is, calculated based on filled sections.

**Post:** Content you share with your network, including text, images, videos, or articles.

**Article:** Long-form content with a title and detailed text, similar to a blog post.

**Endorsement:** When connections validate your skills.

**Mutual Connections:** Connections you share with another user.
